TaskTimer — Privacy Policy

Effective date: Oct 31, 2025

TaskTimer is provided by Erick Oeckel. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices. For questions or requests, contact Erick326598@gmail.com.

Device Permissions

The App may request microphone access only when you choose features that need it (e.g., voice input). Audio is used to perform that feature and is not stored or shared beyond what is necessary to operate the feature. You can deny or revoke this permission in device settings; certain features may then be unavailable.

Information We Collect

  • Account Information: email address, username/display name, and Firebase user ID.
  • Profile & Preferences: avatar configuration (visual settings only).
  • In-App Content: tasks, habits, schedules, completion status, XP/coins, and related metadata you create.
  • Log/Diagnostics: limited technical data (e.g., device model, OS version, timestamps) used to keep the service secure and to diagnose issues.

How We Use Information

  • Provide core functionality (authentication, saving/syncing your data).
  • Personalize your experience (avatar settings, stats).
  • Maintain security, prevent abuse, and debug problems.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

Third-Party Services

  • Google Firebase (Authentication, Cloud Firestore, and optionally Cloud Functions) to store account and app data.
  • Avatar rendering services (e.g., Dicebear or similar) to render your avatar based on your chosen settings.

These providers process data on our behalf under their own terms and privacy policies.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with service providers as described above or if required by law, to protect our rights or users, or with your consent.

Data Retention

We retain your account and in-app content while your account is active or as needed to provide the service. Upon request, we will delete or anonymize personal data unless retention is required by law.

Your Choices & Rights
